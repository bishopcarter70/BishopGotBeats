BishopGotBeats also known as Dayan Jean, Dayanmakebeats is an African-American music producer that was born in Port-au-Prince, Haiti on January 30, 2001. At the age of 9, Bishop moved to Flatbush, Brooklyn NY to live with his father. That was when, Bishop started experimenting with MixCraft, Fl Studio & would spent the remaining of his childhood.While attending Walt Whitman Middle School, Bishop started making beats where he was inspired by Speaker Knockerz, Zaytoven, Young Chop & LondonOnDaTrack.
